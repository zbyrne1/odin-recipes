Nam Pla

Description

Nam pla is a common condiment found at many Thai food restaurants. It combines many of the flavor combinations Thai cuisine is known for, and depending on the recipe, may actually contain all 4 flavor categories: sweet, sour, salty, and savory.

Ingredients

  • Fish Sauce
  • Thai Chilis
  • Lime
  • Garlic
  • Shallots
  • Sugar (optional)

Steps

  1. Dice garlic, chilis and shallots finely
  2. Fill a small mason jar or other sealable container 3/4 full with fish sauce
  3. Add 2 tblsp sugar and the juice of 1 lime, stir until sugar is dissolved
  4. Combine with the previously diced ingredients and done
